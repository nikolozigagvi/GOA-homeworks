Top Cities to Visit

Paris, France

Art, cafes, and iconic views don't miss the Louvre, Montmartre.

Rome, Italy

Ancient ruins and world-class food. Visit the Colosseum, Vatican Museums, and enjoy real gelato.

Barcelona, Spain

Gaudí architecture, tapas bars, and beaches — perfect for culture and leisure.

Amsterdam, Netherlands

Canals, cycling culture, and compact museums — great for short city breaks.

10-Day Sample Itinerary (Classic)

  1. Day 1 to 3 — Paris: Louvre, Eiffel Tower, stroll Le Marais.
  2. Day 4 ot 5 — Amsterdam: Canal tour, Van Gogh Museum, cycle the city.
  3. Day 6 to 7 — Berlin: Brandenburg Gate, museum island, local neighborhoods.
  4. Day 8 to 10 — Rome: Colosseum, Vatican, slow dinners & piazzas.

Tip: Use budget flights and high-speed trains between major hubs to save time.

  • Travel light: One carry-on + daypack speeds you up.
  • Local transport cards: City passes save money for museums & transit.
  • Time your visits: Mornings are best for popular sights to avoid crowds.
  • Currency & cards: Bring a backup card; notify your bank before travel.
  • Local SIM / eSIM: For maps and bookings — or rely on Wi-Fi with offline maps.

Packing Checklist

  • Passport & photocopy
  • Travel adapter (Type C/E/F commonly used)
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Light rain jacket
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Small first-aid kit
  • Light layers (smart-casual outfit for dinners)
  • Portable charger & cables
